Right in the middle — home between Neukölln & Britz

CIVITANO is located where Berlin is currently the most exciting: at the transition from Neukölln to Britz. Urban, lively, international — but a bit quieter than classic Neukölln hustle. Perfect for those who have arrived here and are now looking for their own place.

Restaurants, convenience stores, cafés, playgrounds, parks, daycare — all within walking distance. The Teltow Canal for an evening walk, Hermannstraße for daily life, Hermannplatz for action: everything well connected without being in the middle of the chaos.
